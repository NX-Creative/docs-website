All commands below are client-side and available to any player. Access
is gated in-handler by the active job's `canBill` and `canInspect` flags
defined in [`Config.Societies`](./configuration#societies-job-invoicing)
and [`Config.FreelanceBilling`](./configuration#freelance-billing).

<CommandReference
  name="createinvoice"
  description="Open the invoice-creation menu. Form fields depend on the active job's permissions (society or freelance)."
/>

<CommandReference
  name="invoice"
  description="Alias for /createinvoice."
/>

<CommandReference
  name="inspectplayer"
  description="Inspect the nearest player and surface their outstanding invoices. Intended for law-enforcement jobs with canInspect = true."
/>
