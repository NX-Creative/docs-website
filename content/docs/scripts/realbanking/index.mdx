`nx_realbanking` is a production banking resource for FiveM. It covers
two surfaces that share one backend:

- An immersive **ATM** rendered onto 3D in-world panels (via `nx-3d`)
  with cinematic camera, IK finger input, card-insert animations, and
  per-ATM-model calibration.
- A premium **bank app** for deeper management: accounts, shared
  accounts (business and savings with roles), invoices (society and
  freelance), autopay, VAT, commissions, and an optional credit-card
  system with billing cycles and tier-based limits.

## Features

- **Dual framework.** Detects ESX or QBCore at runtime through the
  `bridge/` layer. Optionally mirrors the framework bank balance onto
  a linked personal account.
- **Multiple account types.** Personal, business, and savings. Shared
  accounts support role-based permissions (`owner`, `manager`,
  `teller`, `viewer`).
- **ATM flow.** Distance-gated session, PIN auth with lockout,
  withdraw, deposit, and transfer with configurable fees, daily
  limits, receipts, and full transaction history.
- **Invoices.** Society invoicing with fine presets per job, freelance
  billing with commission, acceptance flow for personal invoices,
  autopay, VAT breakdown, and overdue handling with late fees.
- **Credit cards.** Tiered cards (Standard, Gold, Platinum) with a
  credit-score model, billing cycles, grace periods, minimum payments,
  ATM cash advance, and a collections threshold.
- **Security.** Server-side validation, rate limits, optional
  teleport, distance, and speed-hack checks, plus Discord webhook
  logging.
- **Localization.** English and Finnish included.
