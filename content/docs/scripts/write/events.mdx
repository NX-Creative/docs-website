---
title: Events
description: Integration surface for nx_write.
---

`nx_write` does not expose net events for third-party resources to
trigger or listen to. The events it registers form an internal protocol
(item-use routing, document open and close, multiplayer animation and IK
sync, placement broadcasts) whose shape is subject to change, so they
are intentionally undocumented.

Integrate through these surfaces instead:

- **The inventory item.** Giving a player a `notepad` (or `postit`)
  through `ox_inventory` and having them use it is the supported way to
  start a writing session. Item use is routed by `ox_inventory`'s own
  `client.event` hook, not by an event you trigger.
- **The `placeFromSlot` export.** See [Exports](./exports) for placing a
  saved note in the world from the item's right-click button.
